# Privacy Policy\n\n**
Effective date: 2025-01-01**\n\n## 

Data We Collect\n
obol stores the following data **locally on your device only**:\n- 
Your risk profile questionnaire answers\n- 
Paper portfolio holdings you add manually\n- 
AI chat history (advisor tab)\n- 
App settings and preferences\n\n## 

Data We Do NOT Collect\n- 
We do not collect personal identification information\n- 
We do not use analytics or tracking services\n- 
We do not sell any data to third parties\n- 
We do not store any data on our servers\n\n## 

Third-Party API Calls\n
When you use AI features, your queries are sent to the AI provider you have selected (Anthropic, Groq, Google, OpenAI, etc). These providers have their own privacy policies.\n\n
Stock data is fetched from public financial data providers.\n\n## 

Your Rights\n
You can delete all app data at any time from Settings > Data & Privacy > Clear all app data.\n\n##
